Collisions - A simple physics simulation of elastic 2D collisions

By Namanyay Goel · Github

Collisions is a quick demo in canvas to show how 2D collisions happen. Created to act as a cool background, the author soon realized its use as physics simulator. Collisions henceforth shows a microscopic view of gas particles.

100% original code. It has a few bugs, but works pretty well.
